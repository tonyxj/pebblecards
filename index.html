Customizable Cards

Add cards that contain the information you care about, such as headlines from your favourite RSS feed, your next Google calendar appointment, local weather, stocks, a world clock, and even a simple note. Customize your cards from within the Pebble App on your smartphone.

Always Visible

The date, time, and card are always visible on the Pebble, without having to press a single button. Use the up and down buttons to cycle through all your cards. Press the middle button to refresh the current card. Hold the middle button to see more details.

Pebble Firmware 2.0

Pebble Cards uses the new JavaScript API in Pebble firmware 2.0. This means there's no companion app to install, and it's compatible with both iOS and Android devices. Everything runs within the official Pebble App.

Settings Screen

Easily add, change, and remove cards from your smartphone. Access the settings screen through the offical Pebble App. Change the watch display to day, night, or automatic mode. Get a vibrate notification on the hour. Sign in with your Google account to access the Calendar card.
